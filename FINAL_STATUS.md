## 🚀 **API Endpoints Ready**

### **AI Features:**
- `POST /api/ai-tutor/session/start` - Start AI tutoring
- `POST /api/ai-tutor/session/chat` - Chat with AI
- `POST /api/smart-content/course-outline` - Generate courses
- `POST /api/smart-content/quiz-questions` - Generate quizzes
